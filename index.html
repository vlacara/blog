The Evolution of New Media:
From ARPANET to Artificial Intelligence

By Jobelle Flores • 30 November 2025 • 12 minute read

1. Defining New Media in 2025

New media is interactive digital content created, distributed, and consumed through networked technologies. Unlike traditional media, it is participatory, hyperlinked, and constantly evolving — transforming every user into a potential creator and publisher.

2. A Visual Timeline of Transformation

1969 – ARPANET
